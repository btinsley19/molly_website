Molly Tinsley

Country Artist

About

Molly Tinsley is a 19-year-old country singer-songwriter known for emotionally honest storytelling and melodies that linger. Originally from Menlo Park, California, she discovered her love for music at an early age, turning a lifelong passion into her calling.

Before fully committing to music, Molly played Division I soccer at Loyola Marymount University, where she developed the discipline, resilience, and drive that now fuel her artistry. The youngest of seven in a close-knit family, she draws deeply from real life, faith, and personal experience when writing.

Inspired by artists like Megan Moroney, Taylor Swift, Chris Stapleton, and Ella Langley, Molly creates music that is intentional, heartfelt, and unmistakably her own. Now focused on writing and recording original material, she decided to move from Los Angeles to Nashville to chase her dream in the heart of country music.
